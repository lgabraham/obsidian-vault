
  Rules

- Never lift immediately after prolonged flexion (rower, bike, sitting)
- Insert a decompression buffer before transitions
- Treat “jarring” as a yellow light for 48–72 hours
- Square up before lifting anything awkward
- No surprise yanks, ever

Exercises

- Dead bugs with perfect contralateral control
- Pallof presses
- Side planks
- Suitcase carries
- Slow hinges with offset load
