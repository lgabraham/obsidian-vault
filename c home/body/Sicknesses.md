Cold (0-2 per year, variable
* comes from stress, over-exercise, travel deregulation or exposure
* Lasts 3-7 days
* Often around temperature changes

Flu/other (0-1 per year)
* comes from exposure 

Sinus infection (0-2 per year, variable)
* comes from tail end of cold
* Lasts 10-14 days 
* Lower energy, nasally, poorer sleep
* Netti pot + rest

Allergies (1 month / year)
* usually late March through April
* Persistent 
* Risk of becoming sinus infection
* Flonase helps
