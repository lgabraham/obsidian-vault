Cycling v02 max

Required modifications
- More upright than race position
- Stay seated (no surging)
- Short intervals only (20–40s)
- Cadence high, torque moderate

The “permission structure” that keeps you saf
You can do VO₂ if all 3 are true:
1. No back awareness during intervals
2. No sensitivity afterward
3. No next-day stiffness spike

If any fail → that modality is paused, not pushed.

Frequency while healing
- Once per week max
- Separated by at least 72 hours
- Easy days before and after
