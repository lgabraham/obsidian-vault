# Anger, withdrawal, and Danny's absence
Danny told me he was going away for 4 weeks in November. I bottled up anger, withdrew, in his words “shut down” and in mine “felt mopey”. 

Anger experienced separately felt unsafe where as anger experienced when “merged” felt safe.

I recalled my parents bonding by expressing anger at others but then anger when separate (esp towards one another) felt unsafe. 

I told him at the beginning it was as if he was trying to have it all. I imagined I was jealous of him so clearly stating what he needs. He told me he was trying to pass this on. I mentioned my trial week of work starting next week.
