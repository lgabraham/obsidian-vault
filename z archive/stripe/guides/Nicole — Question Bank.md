# Nicole — Question Bank

_Cold-recall practice. Just the questions Nicole might ask — no answers. Cover the answer with your hand, say your response out loud in ~30 seconds, then check yourself against [[Nicole — Drills]]. Mix the order each pass so it doesn't go rote._

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why Stripe?
3. Why sales — and why now?
4. Why this team specifically?
5. You first interviewed for the other team (~15 accounts). Why this ~80-account team?
6. Be honest — won't you get bored in a couple of months?
7. What's your 3–5 year vision?
8. How would you approach a book of 80 accounts?
9. Where do you expect to struggle, or need the most coaching?
10. What did you take from the earlier process, and why come back?
11. What does "user impact" actually mean to you in this role?
12. Tell me about a time you turned revenue or customer data into a decision.
13. How do you prioritize when everything feels urgent?
14. What would your last team say it's like to work with you?
15. Why should I believe a three-time founder will thrive being managed, on a team?
16. What's the most complex deal you've run, and what made it hard?
17. How do you handle a no — from a prospect, or internally?
18. What questions do you have for me?
