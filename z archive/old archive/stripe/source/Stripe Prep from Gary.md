High level tips

* Consider it a trial
* Consistently look for:
	* collaborative
	* communicate
	* users first

* ask clarifying questions
* share overall thought process, get to why

* Do research on everyone
	* Use the last 5 minutes to tailor questions to people
	* Solutions architect -- what's a challenge they overcame, what has worked before

4 Interviews:
* Operating principles
	* Accountable, urgency + focus
	* resilient, meticulous in craft, problem solving
	* times you had to be scrappy, made decisions w/o all the data, you had to change a deal or your focus, go out of way for a user, get influence cross functional
	* focus should be on my behavior + impact
	* STA(yours)R
	* You'll be asked 3-5 questions
	* Keep answers to 3.5-4 min mark
	* Feel free to put it back on the interviewer to make sure the interviewer got what they needed
* Functional interview: the case
	* presented with everything you need at the start of the interview
	* looking for:
		* analytical
		* strategic thinking
		* strategic communication
	* Your the CEO of a B2B company
	* 4 things
		* how do you understand a healthy business
		* how to evaluate business
		* how do you breakdown + analyze a problem
		* how do you then formulate and communicate out at an executive level
	* Take any and all notes, think about it how you would prospect a user
		* ask any and all clarifying questions
	* The only thing to prepare is BRING A SHEET OF PAPER
	* Why you chose charts and data
* Functional interview: the technical aptitude
	* Interviewed by a member of the solutions architect team
	* Ask you to give a summary of stripe at a company level
	* Explain in a way that both audiences (technical + non) can grasp what you're saying
	* Hear about your ability to build trust internally and externally
	* How do you enable yourself to have technical conversations with someone who is more or less technical than you
	* 3 stripe products -- lean towards the connect
		* hear your understanding of the product, how it works, what challenges they would have
	* #1 resources is TECHNICAL DOCUMENTATION on Connect 
	* Check your knowledge on APIs, Webhooks, metadata both from a technical view and how they relate back to the process

* 3 things this team looks for
	* enterprise sale experience
	* technical aptitude 100 + 200 level questions
	* strategic + analytical sale, measured thinking
	* sense of scrappiness, resilience
