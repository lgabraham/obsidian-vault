# User Interview

How did you hear about us?
What did you use us for?
How would you describe Spoken?

Kendra
* Lives in Florida, director of a pre-school, home was flooded, moving soon. 
* Seems to be a well informed shopper — knows AllModern is of Wayfair
* Has long been aware of whitelabelling, makes shopping confusing 
* How did you hear about us? Preston’s reel (I think our ad)
* What did you use us for? Looking for specific items.
* She likes lists
  * Browses other sites (and spoken) for inspiration, makes list of things she likes 
  * She uses spoken to search for specific things 
* She is “On the Wayfair app all the time”
  * Likes the variety
  * Likes to create lists
  * Likes the close outs + recent “open box” options
  * It’s difficult to wade through inventory
* She values deals above all else
  * Emphasized Wayfair’s deals 
  * informed me IKEA just introduced steep discount second on site
* Does Spoken’s have an app? Was unaware of our mobile version
* Mostly uses spoken on the desktop 
* She wishes we had more inventory, and especially more inventory in her price range.
* Considers our aesthetic “modern and elevated”
* “No one I know shops at Urban, etc… I bought my couch at Target”
* Spoken a “game changer” if we expand
