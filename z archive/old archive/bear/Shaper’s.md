# Shaper’s

Expanding the Circle — A Proposal

A friend of mine — a fellow founder and Shaper, who I know some of you also admire and call friend — recently expressed interest in joining our circle. I hold him in high regard and think he’d bring quality attention and compelling energy to our lucky crew.

I recognize how much trust we’ve built within our existing circle, and I don’t take this lightly. I’m proud of what we’ve created; I hold it dear. I also find myself curious — even excited — about what a new voice could add, especially someone who is so well aligned with our group. 

Tactically, we’re sometimes light on numbers, and even the max (7 + Z) still seems intimate. 

I’d love to hear how this idea lands for y’all, as it is important to me each of you consents.

Would you be open to welcoming another?
