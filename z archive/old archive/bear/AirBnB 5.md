Direction for Starting a Fire In the Indoor Fireplace
* Take a piece of newspaper and a tear it into 4 sections
* Crumble up the newspaper and place it in the center of the fireplace toward the back
* Take kindling and build it into a teepee shape above the newspaper
* Light the newspaper and push the door to, DON’T latch it yet
* Let the kindling work it’s way into a decent fire, then place a log on top
* Push the door to again until the log is burning really well, once the log is burning well, you can then latch the door
* Slide the air control lever above the door all the way to the right for maximum heat output, slide it to the left to minimize the heat output
* Once you start a fire DO NOT TOUCH THE STOVE, it gets very, very hot
