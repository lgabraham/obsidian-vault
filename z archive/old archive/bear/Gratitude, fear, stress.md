# Gratitude, fear, stress
I am in gratitude for all that is, and all that I would say that I “have” — this body, this mind, these relationships, the spaces I occupy, the things I collect. 

When I worry about the future, often it is because I am thinking from a place of fear, rather than from a place of trust and abundance. 

(Eu)Stress as in lifting something heavy or doing something hard (deliberate, sincere effort) and stress as in anxiety (fear for my safety) are not the same thing. The latter frays my nervous system. The former strengthens me.
