Business
Why is now the right time to do this?
post-covid19, talent crisis in healthcare has created massive gaps in workforce needs that require a solution like ours. Hospitals more than ever before are desperate to work with community colleges and vocational schools to build talent pipelines
What are the alternative solutions available to these hospitals today?
it's a mix of largely legacy, traditional solutions like staffing agencies and laborious in-house efforts to engage 1:1 with community colleges via phone/email/career fairs
What adjacent market looks interesting for future expansions/pivots?
next step would be moving into manufacturing or sustainability/green jobs market segments, which also rely on technical and vocational schools to train the next generation of middle-skill talent
