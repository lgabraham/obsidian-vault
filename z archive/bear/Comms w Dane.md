# Comms w Dane
To be wholly transparent, my predominate feeling right now is simply that I’m scared. To proceed is to change myself, us, and much in my life in ways I cannot now know. 

Almost everything about the prospect of this is wonderfully terrifying. Accepting bids of support from people I respect, so that we might give it all on the field. And maybe fail. Or maybe build something that defines our lives, the lives of others. Possibly, or course, just something middling in between. :)

Part of me is exhilarated by this all, of course. Like the moment before heading out onto stage. It reminds me of the quote by Marriane Williamson. “Our deepest fear is not that we are inadequate...”

Part of me is just scared because I enjoy the comfort of the life I have built, and to give my all is to trade some of this. Another part of me knows that to live a life of meaning, I must be scared in this way sometime, sooner or later. Is now the time? Is this the way?

These are my feelings upon initial digestion. I will keep you near my mind’s movements.
