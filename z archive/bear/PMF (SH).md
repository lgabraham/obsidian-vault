# PMF (SH)

: just ask users “how would you feel if you could no longer use the product?” and measure the percent who answer “very disappointed.”


After benchmarking nearly a hundred startups with his  [customer development survey](https://pmfsurvey.com/) , Ellis found that the magic number was 40%. Companies that struggled to find growth almost always had less than 40% of users respond “very disappointed,” whereas companies with strong traction almost always exceeded that threshold. 

## We then emailed these users a link to a  Typeform  survey asking the following four questions:

[Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/)
1. How would you feel if you could no longer use Superhuman? A) Very disappointed B) Somewhat disappointed C) Not disappointed
2. What type of people do you think would most benefit from Superhuman?
3. What is the main benefit you receive from Superhuman?
4. How can we improve Superhuman for you?
