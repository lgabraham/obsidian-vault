# Affirmations
Be here now. 

I need less than I might think. 

I am love. I am grace. I am peace. I am joy. 

I am home, and I am free.

I am resourceful, creative, whole. 

Good things happen to me, and good things happen through me.

Seek peace. But also do epic shit. 

If not now, when?

What will you do with this one wild and precious life?
