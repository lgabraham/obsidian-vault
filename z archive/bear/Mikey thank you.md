# Mikey thank you

My friend, it really is difficult to imagine that you could be more beautiful inside than out. When I’m feeling insecure, I much rather fancy you a nature-blessed dolt. Alas, your inside (most often, not always — don’t let it go to your head) exceed your outsides, which already set an impossible bar. And so it is a gift to know you. 

Thank you for the comforter. We’re looking for homes now. This can go on our new marital bed. Sleek shipping, too. I’d lost my eye mask the evening before opening it. 

I’ll look forward to the next time our paths meet. It’s always fun.
