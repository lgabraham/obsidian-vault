# Jenn
When I lived with Jenn, I became accustomed to ending a day with her probing, empathetic curiosity. 

She was my roommate at a moment I experienced great loss. For all her ferociousness, she could immediately, softly step inside my head or heart to hold me, or to gently nudge me. And Once she did, it seemed that her ferociousness became my loyal shield. 

I have cannot conceive of the turmoil that was the context for Jenn’s coming of age. But I can appreciate her deep pride in self as one of the primary examples in my life of a beautiful soul arising from crucible of great challenge.
