# Paternity 2


**For the next two weeks, I’m stepping into paternity leave.**
I need this time to be present — for my family, and to reconnect with myself. I’ve realized that carrying the anxiety of the business while also trying to rest doesn’t serve anyone — it only drains me further and makes a clear decision harder down the road.

That said, I’m open to **1–2 mission-critical fundraising calls per week**. I appreciate how I have seen you aim to protect this boundary with me. I’m not abandoning ship. I’m hope to return with clarity and strength.

When I’m back, if we haven’t made meaningful traction toward raising $750k, I’ll be ready to have the hard conversations about how we close this out responsibly and with respect for all we’ve built.

In the meantime, and I say this also to myself, I choose **presence over pressure.** That’s the kind of dad, cofounder, and human I want to be. 🙏
