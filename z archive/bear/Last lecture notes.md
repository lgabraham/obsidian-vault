Socrates 
- independence
- conscience: Self-knowledge + commitment to good

His goal is to keep his soul awake — keep his independence and conscience awake — through *questioning*. 

Iconic model of a man living the examined life. Keeping his soul atuned to the highest values (virtues). He makes it clear he does not have answers to virtues. 

The teacher who teaches without teaching. 

He believes we are all atuned to what is really good — and good for *us*.  

A powerful intuition about an absent beauty. There is something attractive pulling at the soul. You don’t know exactly what it is. But you are working towards getting closer and closer to it. Which leads to a passionate devotion. To those that don’t understand what’s going on, it may seem like madness. 

What Socrates finds most clearly is his limitations — ignorance and death. Fundamental limitations from which we cannot escape. 

The wonderful thing about ignorance is that it gives you something to work on the whole of your life. A beauty of philosophy is that it gives you solid questions that won’t be ruined by anyone else’s answers. The questions endure, don’t worry. 

Good questions last. 

The questions can be an important part about finding meaning in your life. 

You can give meaning to your life by recognizing these human limitations and embracing them with a positive spirit. 

Socrates declares a war between philosophy and poetry?? Paul is drawn in on both sides.

Abnormal conditions raise the question of the meaning of life. 

1. Paying attention makes meaning. 
2. Making beauty and appreciating beauty makes meaning. 
3. The search for knowledge. (Adventure.)
4. An activity in accordance with human excellence — intrinsically valuable, in our control, and distinctly human (reason + emotion, ie love). Action (complete at some time meeting some criterion) vs activity (complete at any point). If you live a life as an activity, it is always complete. 
5. “The base and finale for all metaphysics is friendship” Friendship gives meaning to life. Meaningful things are extra meaningful with friends.
