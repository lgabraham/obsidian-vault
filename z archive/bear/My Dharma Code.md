# My Dharma Code
I play wholeheartedly;
My breath is full, balanced, easy 
My mind is empty, focused;

I act with intent, in flow, finding courage, freely, allowing for serendipity. 
I am curious, learn voraciously, am excellence in process;

I am intuitive. I feel deeply, here and now.
I initiate. I take risks, fail, and persist, embodying resilience. 

I am power. I am a good leader, good follower, engaged participant,  

I choose intimacy; I am a committed friend and partner;
I love and am loved; 

I walk tall on an epic hero’s journey with reverence, divinity, and quiet pride. 

I give myself permission for exctasy, comfort, and healing. 

I recognize the divinity of others; I offer generously, without expectation. 

I eat simple food, and know hunger. 

Food, togetherness, contribution, meditation; and sleep are my medicine.

Even when the milieu is not, I am peaceful, sane, healthy, and whole;
