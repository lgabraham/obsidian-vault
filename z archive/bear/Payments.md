# Payments

I spoke with a very nice person who is a PM for payments at eBay. He suggested the primary reason to manage payments — easiest he suggested was GooglePay + ApplePay — is to get people to come back. In other words, payments as retention! Just something I hadn’t considered. 

One possible way we could implement payments would be to only do it for a limited set of inventory. For example, I have these friends that keep insisting one of a kind used furniture can be found at consignment stores in SF where they pay up to 50% commissions to move the furniture. And this inventory likely isn’t on the internet today. Does several interesting things for us strategically (unique data, larger chunks of revenue per item)
