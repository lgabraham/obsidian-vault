# Trapezoid

Pros
- Nice guys, seemingly great relationship
- Excited about the space with a lot of experience
- Hacky energy — great product-lead marketing idea (email trucking companies w/ some, not all data)
- Efficient hardware + software solution with integrations, differentiated hardware w/ high resolution photos
- I’m not worried about the market size if they can get enough penetration in the warehouses — seems like strong network value

Cons
- 5/6th (CEO) / 1/6th (CTO) equity split
- Young CTO, “grateful to be here” maybe more green / less fire than I’d like to see?
- Not sure I understood him to have an industry vision that seems singular
- $12K/yr for their solution (they say $100K ACV for multi facility) i.e. they have to achieve distribution without a big sales team
- $3K setup cost (baked into first year)
- Seems like there is not a lot of upside to a facility (only 1 gate), i.e. there are not larger facilities with larger ACVs
