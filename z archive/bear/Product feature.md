# Product feature

I have been thinking about Mihai’s insistence of humans in the loop, the constraints of my bandwidth, the untapped potential of our users with regards to data, and the ability to open a dialogue with users. 

Given all of these things, I feel bullish on the following product feature. 

Inviting users to participate in Spoken

Benefit to user 
They get to feel as though they are participating in righting a wrong / helping others. 
They get to build confidence in that we care about accuracy 
They get to an outlet when they have a bad experience.

Benefit to Spoken
We get a more accurate, uniquely differentiated data process. 
We build trust with users. 


User Flow
User clicks on an offer, opens a tab
Return to tab, a modal appears

Modal Flow

Is everything you saw correct?
Yes, no, bye

Great! Thank you for helping other Spoken users get the best deals.  

Embarrassing. What was off?
Price, missing, out of stock, wrong product, other, I’m done

Thank you for helping other Spoken users get the best deals.  

User logic

Limit to 1 of every 5 offers
If user selects bye twice in a row, sleep for 10 offers
If user selects bye 4 or 4, never return 

Offer logic,
Prioritize highest trafficked offers
If 50%+ are wrong, drop offer

Roll out

Modal, unconnected to data
Track usage
Hook up to data
