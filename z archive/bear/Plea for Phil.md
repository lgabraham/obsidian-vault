# Plea for Phil

I have since regained my footing. I am attending YC in the Summer 21 batch with a dear college friend. I cannot, for the life of me, find the paper Phil gave me with the email address of his assistant. Thus is the chaos of death.

This interaction with Phil was, perhaps, my most meaningful moment at GSB. I am devastated that I have not been able to find that email address. It occurred to me that maybe y’all can help.

I don’t ask this lightly. I will do whatever leg work you direct me towards to get in touch with Phil.

Thank you very much for your time.
