# Trust, Power, Dominance
Trust - built through warmth & competence, by adding value, and communicating you can have and advance the group’s interest. Note that expressing and advocating your own interest clearly is also respected. 

Power - the ability to influence someone’s behavior against their will via authority or else wise 

Influence - the ability to motivate someone or affect their behavior by the way you make them feel

Dominance - perceived high place (or high behavior) in social hierarchy

Compliant workforce vs engaged workforce.
