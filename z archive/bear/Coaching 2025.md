# Coaching 2025


Increasingly, I think about our time not as hours spent, but as a longer engagement. My own coach, who I began working with this year, structures our payments with this in mind, and I think it makes sense. 

So for 2025, I propose we move to quarterly billing. I imagine this working as follows.

* Quarterly bill for 12 50-min sessions at our set time (ie 1 off week)
* Pro rated sessions for additional sessions I miss (2 week’s notice)
* Reasonable attempts to reschedule with at least 1 week notice 

In effect, this will have 2 upshots: (1) a commitment to 12 sessions and (2) shifting to responsibility for payment for “late” cancellations (within 1 week). 

I also propose a rate of $330 ($15 or 5% increase). The resulting quarterly bill would be $3,900. 

My goal is to continue our activity in a way that protects the value of my time and commitment to you. 

Finally, as unpaid “paternity leave” of 6 weeks.
