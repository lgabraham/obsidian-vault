# TEF
[The Explorer Fund](https://theexplorer.fund/) is a pre-seed fund by Stanford founders who invest super early in tenacious founders chasing big goals. We invest $100,000 in pre-seed rounds. 

We pride ourselves in bringing a founder perspective to your cap table, being emotionally present, and sharing our experience and extensive network to help you as we can. After raised $60M+ for my current start-up, I found that the founders on the cap table were our best advisors.

Why? 

•⁠  ⁠As entrepreneurs ourselves, we are excited by your 0 to 1 journey. Three of us built and sold our first companies and are deep into our next projects..

If you have the grit to chase big ambitions, or if feel you are the best team to solve a problem, we would love to connect. You don’t have to be fund-raising. We just love talking to fellow founders.
