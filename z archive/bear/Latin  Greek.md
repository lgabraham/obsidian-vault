# Latin / Greek

I know I’ve told you this before, and it’s a bit of naval gazing, but I so like our dual:

omnia vincit amor / love conquers all
epimeleia tes psyches / care of the soul

I love that we chose them independently, that they are both ancient but one Greek and one Latin, that they reflect who we are, and that they amplify one another. Makes me think of us as certain kinds of love warriors, shield and sword to outside evil, leaning into one another. Very romantic.
