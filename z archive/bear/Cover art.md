# Cover art
Hi all - to make it easier for people to get cover art without relying on the bot, I created a Colab notebook that'll generate cover art for you:
https://colab.research.google.com/drive/106N7Ov2sawh3vliRVT8SzkwXo_hx6Nrk?usp=sharing

You need a Google account and Google will give you a free GPU to experiment with. Just follow the instructions to step through the cells.

For free accounts it'll be slow (about ~1 hr to generate), but if you upgrade to the paid Google Colab service you should get a faster GPU and faster generation. I'm hoping a few folks will do that and can help others generate cover art faster.

Definitely play around with it and see what sorts of cool things you can do! Also feel free to improve/remix the notebook and share alternatives
