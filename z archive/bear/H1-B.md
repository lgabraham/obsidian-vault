# H1-B

**H1B Visa Process**
* 85K accepted of 500K (20k reserved for for U.S. Masters drawers)
* Held every March (once a year)

**Costs**
* Putting them in the lottery cots $660
* If they are selected in the lottery - $3K legal fees, $3 Government filing fees
* ~$8K in total

**Timeline**
* First 2 weeks of March - submissions
* April 1s - find out if selected
* un 30th to submit the application, 
* October 1st work start

**Next Steps**
* Reach out in January to onboard

**Spouses**
* H1-B spouses and children under 21 get an H4
* Doesn’t automatically give work authorization

**Other Visas**
* TN for Canadians is easy
* L-1s for international companies

**J-1 Student Visas**
* Vetted Organizations (Universities) can issue J-1s
* Other organizations outside of schools have the same authorization for “cultural exchange”
* Can be mapped to an “internships” that last up to 18 months
* The organization gets the J-1 approved and places the student at our company
* The American Immigration Council / [Cultural Exchange](https://exchange.americanimmigrationcouncil.org/)
* Sometimes such a requirement as “212e” the 2 year home residency requirement
