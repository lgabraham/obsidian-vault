# A little something I wrote....


Without a word

When you find yourself at a loss for words, 
do you cease to understand?
If phrase and frame fail to do justice does expression lose the upper hand?

Alphabets, syntax, grammar, and prose
Each serves as best it can
But the world is full of wonders that these tools can’t comprehend 

Listen closely to Mother Earth, her language precedes time
Communicate the way she does and allow her to open your mind 

No words are uttered by the moon when he whispers to the tide
Nor by the mama eagle when she teaches her chicks to fly 

The blossom unfurls her petals to the sun without needing invitation
Her nectar inspires the hummingbird’s dance, a wordless conversation

The humpback learns the waterways through songs without vowel or noun,
A school of fish can move as one, awareness traveling faster than sound 

The tree knows when to shed its leaves when summer turns to fall 
And the bears awakens from hibernation as if a clarion called

But just in case you need a word and you find you’ve hit a wall,
Feel free to make one up instead, it’s your story after all
