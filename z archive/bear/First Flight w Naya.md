# First Flight w/ Naya

Hello! My 8-week old daughter, Naya, just took her first flight! Southwest, of course. Flight #945 from OAK to AUS. My wife Mimosa and I were nervous about how the flight would go. Emily, Mona, and Karyn went above and beyond to make sure we had everything we need. They found us seats together, delivered us multiple drinks. They even gave us a sweet certificate and pin for Naya’s first flight! It was always the case that Naya, given her parents, would likely be a traveler. It has never been clearer to me that, especially as a family, flying Southwest is the only way to fly between the Cities we love! Thank you, Emily, Mona, and Karyn for the wonderful experience! And thank you Southwest for valuing family travel and in general fostering a culture that prioritizes our customer experience.
