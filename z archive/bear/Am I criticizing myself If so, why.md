# Am I criticizing myself? If so, why?

Affair -> deep insecurity -> culture of judgement as compensation -> projection, dominance + control, ego defense, fear of vulnerability 

By elevating themselves above others, people can avoid exposing their own vulnerabilities 

My mother and father had an affair, which lead to my birth. I now understand that the culture of judgement that I knew in my early life likely was a coping mechanism to the internal insecurity of my parents and external social judgement from others.
