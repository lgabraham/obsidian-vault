# USPS Note for John

To whom it may concern,

My name is Geoff Abraham. My wife Mimosa and I inhabit 5398 E Heise Rd.

We hope to restore the Postal Service, which had been provided to this home when owned by Marion Blakely, from late 1950s until 2014 (when my father purchased the home). 

I understand that, as we are just 0.5 miles from Heise Ranch, we are eligible for service. 

In addition to our own service, I would be glad if our good neighbors, the Martin family, were extended their own service. They are excellent citizens of our small community. 

Thank you for your time and attention. 

Respectfully yours,
Geoff & Mimosa
