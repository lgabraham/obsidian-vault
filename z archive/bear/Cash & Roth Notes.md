# Cash & Roth Notes
This is where your focus ought be on an investment strategy. 

A coherent strategy will account for:

Investment Horizon
Risk Tolerance
Cash need

This may seem ambiguous, but these things are at least guessable. For example, you don’t anticipate serious change to your life (cash need) outside of an admittedly expensive lifestyle for perhaps 10 years. Much may change in this time — primarily deaths that affect available cash — and a primary goal of the interim will likely be something that results in income to offset expenses. 

Perhaps most important: understanding what you want from life, then aligning all of the above to this — that is the primary strategic question. You must start there, accepting the answer will be imperfect, and may change.
