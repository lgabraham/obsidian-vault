# PsychoPomp


Y’all, Mimosa and I did this yesterday. It transported me. Standard tickets are $130 for 2, which is pricey. Yet you support the arts — one of the players is a friend of mine from college who is a rare breed that makes a living in SF exclusively as an actor — and you return to experiencing the joy of (interactive) theatre. It runs through June 27th. Great for couples. Go before it gets discovered!
