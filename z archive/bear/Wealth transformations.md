# Wealth transformations

If wealth is the set of transformations you can enact, then:

1. My wealth is always (not necessarily) increasing on account of the work of my living cohort + the arrow of progress. My future self is wealthier than my present self given the same purchasing power. Even though my wealth relative to my peers may not be. 
2. It seems there must be an intentionality or at least directionality for the wealth to have meaning. Otherwise it is merely an increase in potential. What *kind* of transformations do I want to perform?

Wealth without agency is not wealth at all. Agency can replace wealth.
