Thematic thesis
We believe the internet is to today as electricity was to 1900. Electricity was first harnessed as constant light in 1835. Edison patented the light bulb in 1880. If you were to live in the year 1900, you might have believed you had witnessed a once-in-a-lifetime revolution. Yet the magnitude of change in the first 60 years of electricity’s history was dwarfed by what transpired in the following 60 years, as every industry adopted electricity in ways that would transform its companies.
The internet was born in 1983. We might fancy ourselves as having witnessed a modern miracle since then, with the birth of social networks, ecommerce giants, . We would be missing the plot. By 2050 every company will be tech-enabled in a way that transforms them.. Our fund will focus on ideas that enable legacy industries to adopt new technology.
