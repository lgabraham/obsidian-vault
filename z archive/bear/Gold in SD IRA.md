# Gold in SD IRA
The safest approaching to holding IRS approved bullion is with an approved depository. However, many retirement investors have looked into potentially holding precious metals (gold, silver, palladium bullion) in a safe deposit box at a U.S. bank in the name of the Self-Directed IRA LLC is that in the “physical possession” of a U.S. trustee or bank. Although, there may be sound support for this position there has been no IRS guidance on this issue.
