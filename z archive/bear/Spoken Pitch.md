# Spoken Pitch
Hi, I’m Dane and with my co-founder, Geoff, we’re building Spoken.

Spoken is a website where you can discover and buy anything. We’re starting in furniture.

We launched 4 months ago. Have over 50,000 monthly active users, and we’re growing at 135% per month.

Our users were frustrated by furniture shopping, where deceptive practices are the norm.

Retailers rename products and disguise details to prevent price comparison. 

We find the exact same product at multiple stores to give you the best buying options.

We’re starting in furniture because the market is fragmented. Many sellers on many sites sell a relatively small number items. Until Spoken, you couldn’t search all products in one place.

Online Furniture is a $50B market growing 20% annually.  Ecommerce is a $1T market growing 15% annually.

My co-founder, Geoff, is a Stanford MBA grad and sold his previous company.

I’m a two-time technical YC founder. I also sold my last company. And, I spent the last 5 years as the VP of Platform at Greenhouse.

We are Spoken. Thank you.
