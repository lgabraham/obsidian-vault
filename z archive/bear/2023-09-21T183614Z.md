### Optimizing Sleep Quality with Smart Gadgets
Besides decorating your bedroom for relaxation, the quality of sleep you achieve can also be enhanced using specific gadgets:
* **Smart sleep trackers:** These can monitor your sleep patterns and provide insights on how to improve.
* **White noise machines:** Helpful in blocking out disruptive noises, ensuring a peaceful night's rest.
* **Temperature-regulating devices:** They help maintain an ideal sleeping environment by adjusting the bed's temperature according to your preferences.
