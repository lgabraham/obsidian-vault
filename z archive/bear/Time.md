# Time
It’s so hard to think about time. 

I feel secure on

a 1 year timeline (June 2025)
- child?
- year 3 for Naya
- Spoken matured (needing to raise)

a 2 year timeline (June 2026)
* year 4 for Naya
* Spoken alive? (12-18 more months?)

a 3 year timeline (June 2027)
* kindergarten for Naya or TRIS
* new TRIS entrant
* Spoken alive?

Thinking beyond a year often collapses to “infinity” with all of its associated pressures

Things not considered here:
* deaths
* windfalls
* great fortune

I write this from Vierzon, where JeF is so, so happy to have his family here. This place is full of life, because of him. It makes me miss my father. And it makes me wonder where I would be so happy to greet my family. 

Family arrivals and departures are so full of love. How beautiful they are. 

This was the magic of Idaho, too. My Dad’s soul there. While I wonder when I can honor my history, like JeF honors his here, I recognize that river bend is so much more lifeless without my Dad, and it did not give me life to be there. 

It does give me life to be in nature. Something I have named though not fully embraced. I wonder whether I need to “own” the land to enjoy it. No doubt there is something special about that. 

In truth, I feel “placeless”. So many places I enjoy — Albuquerque, Austin, New York, Jackson Hole, our Bay. Mexico, Italy, Japan. Perhaps there is such a joy as an endless rotation through these. 

Many more questions to ask and have resolved in the next 3 years without expending too much energy on this. :)
