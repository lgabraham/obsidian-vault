Entrepreneurship is a mouthful. To me, the word approximates aspiration — where I aim to marry how the world is with how I hope it will be. Small and strange corners of the world, yes — tricycles and credenzas — whose puzzles enliven me.

Enlivening, too, for me, is how entrepreneurship is shared. Working with epic partners is one of the greatest joys of my life (cc Mimosa, Dane). Encouraging others to find their deep partners and their strange and enchanting worlds is another source joy.

Rish’s inspires me to embrace my highest frequency. Marcin’s inspires me to fall in love with our world, again and again. Andrew inspires me to American idealism, even when some of its sheen has dimmed. 

While I continue building Spoken, I am excited to deepen my partnership with friends I respect, and to better enable others to find their highest aspirations. Onward.
