# Note to Dane

Dane,

I want to put a few things in writing before I step away for paternity leave.

I’m exhausted — the uncertainty we’ve lived in for the past 7+ months has taken a toll. I feel anger that we haven’t completed the fundraise before I leave, and also feel determined to protect time with my family for our time in Canada. I plan to fully unplug July 10–26. I need that space.

I’m also feeling doubt. It’s become harder to believe that this business can support my family or justify the personal cost. I’m not making any immediate decisions, but I want to be honest: I will need to set clearer boundaries when I return — around how funded we need to be and how much more uncertainty I can personally carry. I’ll use this time to reflect and recharge, and I’d love if you used it to reflect too — on what conditions would make it right to keep going, and what lines we shouldn’t cross, financially or emotionally.

This doesn’t mean I’m walking away. It just means I need to see things clearly, and I want us to have the courage to make grounded decisions — together or separately — when I return.

Grateful for you,
Geoff
