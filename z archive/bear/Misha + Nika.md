# Misha + Nika

I showed up to this weekend for love — for your love. And I am leaving deeply moved by it. 

Something that struck me about the context of your love is the idea that y’all may not have *many* clear models of North Star, deeply committed loving partnerships. (Forgive me if this isn’t true. I met many lovely couples. Further, I mean no offense to Jeff or Charles.)

If I’m displacing, it’s because I relate to this. I therefore find the fire with which your love burns — your commitment to partnership, family and all that this entails — more unique and inspirational. 

Thank you for so boldly asking me (and us) to join you in a journey that means so much to you, starting in a place that means so much to you. (I won’t forget the morning beauty.) I vow to do my part to support the whole enterprise. 

As I’ve told you, Misha, you remind me much of my grandfather. It’s not lost on me that he also happened to be the first man to show me what epic romantic and familial love meant. 

May you continue to light the way to love in a deep and committed way, even when the apparent models are not so many or so near. 

May we inspire one another to cultivate such a love that becomes an anchor of community that in turn anchors us.
