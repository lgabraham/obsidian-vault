# Bringing Sanity to Furniture Buying

Buying physical things online involves a host of considerations, like double-checking dimensions, quality checking items without touching the item, evaluating credibility of sellers, etc. 

Buying furniture online is a special case of hard. In no one location can you quickly and easily search all the furniture that is sold. When you can search furniture in a limited way, it is difficult to compare like with like — renaming the same exact item or dynamically generating proprietary images. This is true, even though the number of furniture makers and the items they make is relatively small in the universe. 

Finally, many search experiences aren’t built with the understanding that many of us don’t even know what we want, or how to begin to understand this. What is the difference between a credenza and a buffet, anyway?

Spoken is built to solve these problems. We let you compare like with like across all domains of the internet, even when stores try their hardest to fool you. We will also let you search for furniture in ways you never have before. 

We are building this because we love the act of making a space a home, and we love solving hard problems. As we all become more open to buying furniture online, we believe in making furniture search as intuitive and sane as possible.
