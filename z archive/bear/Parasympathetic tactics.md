# Parasympathetic tactics

To engage the parasympathetic nervous system, focus on these key practices:

1. **Deep Breathing:** Slow, diaphragmatic breathing (e.g., 4-7-8 technique).
2. **Mindfulness Meditation:** Regular meditation to promote calm.
3. **Progressive Muscle Relaxation:** Tense and release muscle groups.
4. **Yoga/Tai Chi:** Combine movement with breath control.
5. **Cold Exposure:** Brief cold showers or facial immersion.
6. **Massage Therapy:** Relaxing massages to reduce stress.
7. **Nature Time:** Spend time in calm natural settings.
8. **Laughter/Social Connection:** Positive interactions and laughter.
9. **Acupuncture:** Stimulates parasympathetic activity.
10. **Good Nutrition/Sleep:** Balanced diet and quality sleep.
11. **Gratitude Practice:** Reflect on positive aspects of life.
