# Deals

Pawan at all, something I was tbh inking about this morning is the disambiguation between best deals _on_ a store and best deals _from_ a store. 

One means finding the best deals elsewhere. The other is like shopping a stores clearance rack. Importantly, the latter is good for the stores _and_ may makes more sense for the user on key stores like Amazon and Wayfair. 

We could disambiguate the language with something like:
* best deals from Amazon
* Best deals on Amazon items

I do think I want to see both on a collections page, at least for the biggest stores:
* Amazon
* Wayfair
* Walmart
* Target
* Home Depot
* Lowe’s 

Open to thoughts and discussion here.
