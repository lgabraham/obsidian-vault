# learning
I was initially surprised at how important true empathy seemed in allowing my clients to release their unhelpful stories. 

I am constantly surprised how often overwhelm arises from a sense of isolation, often from our most primary relationships. 

I am also surprised at the integration of our emotional lives. It demands coaching of the whole being, insofar as I am able. 

I am pleasantly surprised at the effectiveness of my intuition. It emboldens me to take risks. 

Both of my clients have another coach. I felt insecure about this once. In time, I realized (of course) that different minds and hearts will exchange different wisdom. This dramatically expands the potential of coaching as an idea, and I wonder why I was threatened. 

I am pleasantly surprised by how the relationships have compounded, and positively in awe of my client’s deepening relationship with themselves. 





I am equally surprised how a deepening trust is salve for all.
