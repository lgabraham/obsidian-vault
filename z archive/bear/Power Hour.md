* After COVID so many more of us work from home so the potential places to find one’s partner are more limited. You met your wonderful life partner, Mimosa, at Cabrio - was it ten years ago? Dating at work feels less and less accessible today. Do you think that in 2022 you and Mimosa would have still dated and developed your relationship?

No. And I think this is what makes us so special.
The sliver of ven diagram overlap was that we liked riding bikes together and were kind of eccentric
We discovered areas of deep values alignment, though we also navigated areas of real tension
When its easy, it feels like it couldn’t be easier, and when it’s hard it feels quite hard
I think our commitment to making us work is precisely makes us strong.
