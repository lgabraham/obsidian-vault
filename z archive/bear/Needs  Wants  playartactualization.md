# Needs / Wants / play/art/actualization

Differentiating wants and needs seems like it can help me.

Some things I feel as needs are wants. 

Wanting the identity of someone who has a 2nd home, and the potential to visit SF in my former home is one level of want. 

Wanting my family to be safe under one roof, my children to have a good education, myself to have space to host, run/cycle, sauna, etc. is another.  

Mistaking wants for needs makes them harder to let go of. 

I need an income. Though not today. I need meaning in work. Or maybe I want it. I want the flexibility and identity Spoken has offered me. I want for Dane and I to feel good about what we’ve built. I want for us to have 12-18 more months to see if we can flame the tinders into a fire. I want to be able to rely on Dane to arrive with energy. Fewer needs here than I might have guessed.

Need — sleep

Want — health (comfort, ease)
Want — good sleep
Want — healthy, delicious food
Want — adventure 
Want — time in nature 

Want — meaning in work

Want — freedom
