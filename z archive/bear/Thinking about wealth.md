# Thinking about wealth

My total wealth approximates $3m without Spoken equity. 

An income of $225k per year seems to support us reasonably. 

I believe my market rate for a non-startup job to be $180k - $250k. 

Within 20 years, I will likely inherit an additional ~$3m, yet this brings up complicated feelings. 

Simply considering $6m, 4% is $240k/yr. 

The main unconsidered expense in my “flows” are Naya’s and Enso’s education. I would gladly work to fund their educations. 

Spoken’s equity is worth somewhere between $0 and $7m today, and could be worth $12m+ in the future, if it works. 

My downside risk seems to be “covered”, Spoken’s equity is a “doubling” bet.

Work is as much about identity as security. 

Thinking about wealth doesn’t seem to do much towards making me feel more secure. 

How to sleep like a baby? Perhaps it is as much about the mechanics — wind downs, meditations, conceptions of self, etc — as it is about wealth building.
