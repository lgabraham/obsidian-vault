1) Would I be joining as a LP at 25k contribution?
   1a) Yes.

2) Does the carry change? Also, I would love to better understand what this means :).
   2a.) “Carry” does not vary by LP (or any other thing). The “management fee” here, given our fund size, is almost wholly paying AngelList to manage the fund. We won’t pay ourselves via such a fee. We earn 20% of the profit. So if your $25K becomes $50K, we return you $45K and earn $5K (20% of the $25K “profit”). 

3) I noticed a few of the investments are in companies started by the GPs. How do you manage conflict of interest?
   3a)These investments were made prior to the fund emerged as an idea in our brains. We note them because they are a part of our personal portfolios. (They are also happen to be performing well. The sum of our personal angel portfolios over 5 years has returned ~2x our initial investments in cash distributions and is currently marked ~5x in notional value. This does not guarantee future performance, of course.) All of these companies are now past the stage of this fund. I don’t anticipate a current partner seeking funding at our stage for the duration of this fund. 

One possible conflict of interest has to do with whether or not we treat this fund as our primary investment vehicle. In other words, are we making personal investments the fund does not have access to. We have all agreed this fund will be our primary investment vehicle. We will bring all opportunities to the fund first.

We likely have not anticipated all the ways conflict of interest can arise.
