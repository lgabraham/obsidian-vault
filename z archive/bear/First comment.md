# First comment
Hi PH friends! Geoff here, co-founder of Spoken.

We’ve always believed that making a house a home should feel inspiring — not overwhelming. But online furniture shopping is often the opposite:
* Everything’s renamed, so comparing prices is confusing.
* It’s hard to describe your style in a search bar.
* Filters and keywords rarely lead to “the one.”

So we built **Explore** — a new way to discover furniture by *vibe*, not filters.

Just click an item you like, and we’ll show you more that *feel* like it. No need to type anything. No menus. Just… more like this.

Explore works on top of Spoken’s price comparison engine, so you’re still getting the best deals across 1,000s of stores — just now in a way that feels more natural and fun.

We’d love your feedback — what do you like, what’s confusing, what should we build next?

Thanks for taking a look.
– Geoff
