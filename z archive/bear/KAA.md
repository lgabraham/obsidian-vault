I would like to take a minute or two to pay tribute to the International Women’s Forum NM and to celebrate the milestone, that the New Mexico Historic Women Marker Initiative  reached today. 
A quick background..

Our state began its official scenic marker program in 1935 to encourage tourism. Seven decades 
later, in 2005 with nearly 500 Markers in place,….women were mention around 2 or 3 times…barely with a footnote.

With that puzzling observation…the power of an idea and vision rapidly flourished into an initiative. 
In 2006 with support of Governor Richardson, his wife Barbara, and the State Legislature, the New Mexico Historic Women Marker Initiative secured, funding to erect roadside markers, celebrating women, whose actions and influence help shape the states history in ways that continue to impact our culture today.

After an intensive statewide nomination and review process,…having received responses from every county, pueblo, tribe, nation in New Mexico…the first women makers began appearing in 2007.

Fast forward, nearly 2 decades later with the unwavering dedication and resilient support of IWF-NM…there are nearly 100 roadside markers up  honoring over 125 women through out the state.
Today, Debbie Martinez, La Chicanta marker…marks a milestone for the initiative started in 2006.
 
In 2022, and 2024 the Historic Women Marker Program received additional funding with the support of Governor Michelle Lujan Grisham  and the State Legislature for public outreach and additional programing.

As of today, with public and private support..and the strong partnership with the divisions  and departments with state government, we have established a K-12 curriculum, digital archives, an interactive website, traveling exhibits, a social media platform and speaker bureau.

While the markers serve as a physical reminder of past accomplishments, the larger goal is to 
Educate, Inspire and Empower present and future generations..with the art of storytelling and the continued recognition and celebration of women past, present and future.
