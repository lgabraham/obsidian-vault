# PPP Language
The Company received a loan via the Payroll Protection Program (“PPP”) on May 8th, 2020 in the amount of $49,666.34. The Company also received a Second Draw from PPP on January 26th, 2021 in the amount of $69,532.89. This Purchase Agreement assumes all of these funds were used by the Buyer according to Federal guidelines, that the loans will be forgiven, and that the forgiveness will be the responsibility of the Buyer. In the event these loans are not forgiven, The Buyer will owe the Seller the residual amount, at terms to be determined by the Buyer and Seller. The Buyer will therefore also claim any and all tax benefits associated with these loans. Any further Federal assistance that occurs after Closing will be the sole responsibility of the Seller.
