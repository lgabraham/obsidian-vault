# Sam

Sam,

Thank you for this beautiful Christmas missive. Pardon my delay in responding. Mimosa and I don’t watch much TV, but we now have Ted Lasso on our short list.

I had been thinking of you recently. Mimosa and I introduced Naya to Aunt Karen in NM this fall. Karen has a photo of you with Constantina at Naya’s age now. It stopped me in my tracks.

I found myself trying to imagine what it must be like to have known her at that adorable age and also witness her thriving as the brilliant, full-grown adult she is today. It must be a kind of disbelief I should hope to one day experience. 

Congratulations on your child finding love. George would be welcome under all circumstances. That he has a family name is not lost on me. I’m glad y’all can relate. He is lucky to count you father-in-law.

As for our Christmas, my Mom joined us for our family’s first Holidays in our Oakland home. We honored some old traditions — a Christmas Eve meal my father would have been proud of — and began some new ones. Save the stresses of COVID, the days could not have been happier. 

For the record, you will always be welcome to pull coin’s out of Naya’s ear. 

While I agree that abstinence can indeed be a source of strength — and I admire your philosophy — I also hope we needn’t diminish our merriment much longer. As do I hope that as the world finds its gaiety again, our growing families will cross paths.

With love,
Geoff
