# Optimism as advantage
Why are most of us pessimistic?

A theory by @naval which beautifully answers the above question:

"When people used to live in jungles, once they heard a tiger's roar. The optimists said that the tiger isn't approaching us but the pessimists said that we are out of here...(1/3)

The pessmists ran away and survived. Hence we are the descendants of pessimists and we are genetically hardwired to be pessimists. But the modern society is far safer and there are no tigers wandering around...(2/3)

So adapting to modern society is overriding your pessimism and taking slightly irrationally optimistic bets because the upside is unlimited and the downside is limited."

-Naval Ravikant (3/3)
