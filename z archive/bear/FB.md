# FB

Family and friends, all: a fun update. I am embarking on a new career chapter w/ Dane. 

Dane and I have been friends for 15 years. Our friendship has consisted primarily in doing hard things together, and challenging one another to do hard things. We’ve each enjoyed building companies before.We decided to build one together.

We named our (legal) company Treehouses, Inc. after a Paul Graham essay. “Kids just plunge in and build their treehouse without worrying about whether they’re wasting their time or how it compares to other treehouses.” Building our company so far has felt like this.

We will to build the best place to buy furniture online. Buying large items without ever seeing them in person — and ending up happy— is more common than ever before. This happens less when buying furniture online. We will change this.

We didn’t expect to build a company around furniture. We began building something else, but not enough people wanted it. But in many ways, this project feels well-aligned to my person. My father, in fact, built several furniture businesses. My family engrained in me the importance of making a space a home. Choosing furniture is central to this act.

Dane is as happy of a partner as I could have ever hoped for. He committed and focused to the point of intimidating me. He is also generous. He didn’t apply one iota of pressure when I took time to be with my new family for Naya’s arrival. We complement one another. We ‘re deeply aligned. Most importantly, we have fun together.

Mimosa is the other partner for whom I am deeply grateful. Her love emboldens me. I would not be able to take such risk in this chapter without her stable support. Even amidst the intensity of building something new, she and I have held to date nights and adventures. I could not have conceived of the fullness of our life. So much of this fullness I owe to her.

Our site is Spoken.io. It is only a fraction now of what we imagine it to become. One way that you can help, if you’ve made it this far, is simply to download and review our Chrome Extension. Or if you know anything strange or interesting about how furniture is sold online, I’m all ears.
