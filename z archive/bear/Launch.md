# Launch

### 1. 1 Week Before Launch
* Finalize launch date (target 12:01am PT).
* Schedule post on Product Hunt.
* Prepare:
  * Product name and tagline (≤ 60 characters)
  * Description (≤ 260 characters)
  * Logo thumbnail (240x240px, ideally a GIF)
  * 2+ product gallery images (1270x760px)
  * YouTube demo video (1–2 min, fast “aha” moments)
  * Tags (3 relevant categories)
  * PH usernames of team members (“makers”)
* Write the first comment (short story + use case + ask for feedback)
* Reach out via Product Hunt’s Intercom chat to request “Featured” status review

⠀2. 3–5 Days Before Launch
* Update PH and LinkedIn profiles for credibility
* Prep launch-specific landing page (or ensure homepage is focused + clear CTA)
* Prime your community:
  * Email list
  * Slack/Discord communities
  * Social posts teasing launch (use “Coming Soon” PH page if scheduled)

⠀3. Launch Day (12:01am PT)
* Verify your launch is on the “Featured” feed — message PH support if not
* Post “first comment” immediately
* Send launch email to your network (link directly to the PH launch page)
* Share across:
  * LinkedIn (founder post + team reshares)
  * X/Twitter (tag PH + key community members)
  * YC backchannels (if relevant)
* Assign team members to:
  * Respond to all comments
  * Monitor and share momentum on social (screenshots, quotes, etc.)

⠀4. Post-Launch (Next 3–7 Days)
* Thank your supporters and share results (X, LinkedIn, email)
* Highlight best feedback/learnings internally
* Claim your Product Hub and update it
* Use badges and social proof (e.g. “#2 Product of the Day”) in marketing materials
* Consider a follow-up blog post or investor update tying in the launch
