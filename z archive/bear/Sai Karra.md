# Sai Karra



I graduated from Stanford Business School in 2018 and am currently cofounder of Spoken.io, a YC-backed startup. I write to express my emphatic support for Sai Karra’s admission to Stanford University.

My 2nd interaction with Sai, less than half my age, involved a difficult business conversation. His proposal for my business had been mispriced. He had to deliver to us the revised, higher price.

Sai handled the matter with total integrity. This impressed me. I have MBA-minted peers who might have yet to speak with equal directness and grace. We awarded Sai and his brother our contract, which had been competitive, even though they were the youngest team by several years.

Sai is currently teaching our company how to navigate its most crucial marketing channel (TikTok). He makes learning fun — for himself and others. It makes perfect sense why he has built a natural following around this. Today he is materially informing our messaging and marketing strategy.

Sai shows unparalleled energy and determination. He listens well. Yet he drives the conversation forward. He is empathetic, creative, and kind. 

I know Sai will build deep learning relationships with his peers and professors. I can’t imagine a better environment for him than this prestigious school.
