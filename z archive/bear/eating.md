**Mindful eating** 

Mindful eating -> hunger and fullness cues. 
If you ignore one, how can you expect to recognize the other?

Nonjudgement: eat when you’re hungry and stop when you’re full without labeling “good” or “bad”

Be aware whether your eating because of boredom, stress, or genuine hunger.

In so many places in my life, I am mindful. It seems to fail consistently with eating. Why? 
1. Allergenic response -> confusion over food. 
2. Using eating to reach for emotional comfort from experience familial love through food. 
3. Fullness / glucose spike as a drug that removes me from emotional pain. 
4. Issues of control, including around pooping, that are kinds of scars from a lack of control in early childhood. 
5. Modeling, ie my parents often labelled their behavior as good + bad, and used eating as a form of self and group southing, and also “treated themselves” ie went out to eat when feeling “good”. 
6. Suppressing my hunger to focus such that it becomes large and makes it difficult to be mindful. 


When I am eating out of stress, can I take a moment to identify and resolve the stress?

When I am eating out of boredom, can I commune with nature?
