# Ruth/Abla

How do the SMBs in your market break down by size? How large are the largest? How many are under 10 employees? For the 2k customers you hope to have after 12 months, what is their median size?

Which of these organization sizes do you see as your “beachhead” market, and who are the decision makers?

How much of your waitlist do you expect to convert to paying customers? If they do convert, what will be your MRR?

What do you imagine to be the ACV of your largest customer contract at scale? Of your median customer contract?
