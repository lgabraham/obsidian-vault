# Seek Wealth, not Money or Status
Wealth is a positive sum game. 

Status is a zero sum game. 

Money wants do be an IOU for work you (or s.o.) did in the past. 

In the past, status has been a better guarantor of wealth. 

The two games are often in tension. 

Some people believe deep down they cannot make money or should not. They play status games. 

Know which game you are playing.

The goal of wealth is sovereignty.
