# Butcher Box
First box:

The packaging was handsome. The materials of the box were impressive. 

The NY Strip was great. I really dislike what I think of as “freezer” flavor in meat. This meat did not have any of this. 

My first thought is that I would like control of (1) what meat I get. The box I purchased things that will take me longer to use and (2) easily pause the box. I have not tried these things yet, and will share my experience when I do. 

Changing experience
