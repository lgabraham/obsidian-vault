J is a man of craft. Uncompromising dedication to excellence in a domain. 

Never would I wish corporate competition to carve such a division down your heart. I wouldn’t wish it on my worst enemy. 

As for excel, he knows where to find cheap terpentine. Macros in and out. 

J has been balancing excellence in disparate domains all his life. He was valedictorian and a baseball star 

It was never his excellence or his like ability. It was his ability to manage all of these things at once. 

A lot of life seemed like weighty trade offs because he knew acutely the loss of each domain. An extra hour at work could close a deal. 

Claire provide stability and perspective. He started finding solutions to his formerly existential problems. Hiring a developer to build macros for mac. 

When J first met Claire, he described her as bubbly bringing him up out of his head. 

Actually she grounds him. Gives him a beautiful home and place of rest. Where the delimnas are not so life altering and the trade offs not so sharp.
