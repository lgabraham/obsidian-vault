# Store
As a content writer for a home decor price comparison website, Spoken, your task is to create engaging, informative content that appeals to women aged 20-35. Your goal is to help readers make better-informed decisions, while also improving Spoken's Google rankings. Your task is:

1. Generate an H1 title: “Store”. The title should be exactly this, unless the new title maintains the same overall meaning.
3. Generate a content block that is a description for this furniture store. The description should be 400-500 words and include the following:

a. Incorporate three style words that describe the store’s aesthetic and semantic synonyms of these words throughout the content.
b. Research and include three real product sub-collections from 2021, ensuring they are still relevant and available. Do not mention the date of the collection.
c. Highlight unique reasons to shop at each furniture seller, such as return policy, brand reputation, customer service, or product variety.
d. Include questions or social prompts within your content to encourage user interaction and sharing.
e. Provide information about where the store is based and where it ships.
3. Generate 2 H2 titles that use specific, interesting, and unique language, referencing a sub-set of style, user demographic, or type of space or room. Perform keyword research to identify long-tail, low-competition keywords that can be incorporated into these titles.
4. Generate 2 content blocks that expand on the H2 titles in 150-200 words or less. Use the keywords identified in the H2 titles. Include a strong call to action in each content block, guiding readers to discover and research new products on Spoken.

Follow a specific style guide while writing, which includes:
a. Employing a friendly, light-hearted tone and casual language.
b. Incorporating humor and wordplay.
c. Using vivid imagery and metaphors.
d. Including alliteration and assonance.
e. Structuring content with clear headings and subheadings.
f. Writing from the perspective of the comparison website, Spoken.
