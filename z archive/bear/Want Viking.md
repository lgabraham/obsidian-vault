# Want Viking

There is a part of me who hates people who as for what they want (Rish, Lief). Why?

They haven’t earned it? What does this mean? What is it to earn a want?

They haven’t earned their privilege? Of course they haven’t. It would be impossible for anyone to earn their privilege. Our lives are good on the backs of so many that came before us. 

They’re not thinking of others? But they can and do. They listen. They simply also speak. Sometimes first. 

I wonder if, like rage, if I open up my wants, there may be more than I realize.
