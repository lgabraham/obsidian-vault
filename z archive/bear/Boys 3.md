So I wrote this memo mostly for myself. We do not have a pitch deck. We may not make one. And ofc so that you could challenge my ideas.

We will take our first outside check today from a friend of Dane’s. Y’all are my first communication. I’m not explicitly asking for your investment here, but I can tell you that I would be more committed to not failing if your names appeared in our cap table.
