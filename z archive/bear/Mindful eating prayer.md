# Mindful eating prayer

Before anything solid 

**Solo prayer**

For the life given to sustain me and the love that surrounds me, I am grateful. May I live in harmony with what is. Give me the courage to make my life a blessing. Amen. 

**Family prayer** 

For the life given to sustain us and the love that now surrounds us, we are grateful. May we live in harmony with what is. Give us the courage to make our lives a blessing. Amen.
