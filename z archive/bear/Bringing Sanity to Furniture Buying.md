# Bringing Sanity to Furniture Buying 

Buying physical things on the internet is hard. You must quality-check something without touching it, double-check dimensions for where the thing will go, and evaluate a sellers’s credibility often with little data. Buying furniture online is a special case of hard. It is *especially* hard. And it is harder for three reasons. 

First, in no one location can you quickly and easily search all the furniture that is sold. This is true, even though the number of furniture makers and the items they make is relatively small.

Next, when you can search furniture in a limited way, it is difficult to compare like with like. Sellers often rename the same exact item or produce different images to the same item. Both of these tactics fool traditional search engines. 

Finally, many search experiences aren’t built with the understanding that you might not know what you want, or how to begin to understand this. What is the difference between a credenza and a buffet, anyway? This more complex because buying furniture can be deeply emotional. It can come to define your home.

Spoken is built to solve these problems. We let you compare like with like across all domains of the internet, even when stores try their hardest to fool you. We will also let you search for furniture in ways you never have before. 

We are building this because we love the act of making a space a home, and because we love solving hard problems. 

As we all become more open to buying furniture online, we believe furniture search should be as intuitive and as sane as possible.
