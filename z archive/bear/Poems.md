Oh my. How he sanctifies finitude. So many gems that will change how I see the world… Summoning the dead. No holiday is holy without ghosts. Secret holidays of the heart. Most of what happens happens beyond words. What must be lost was never lost on us.

Secret holidays of the heart. Secret language that you speak with the people you love.
