# NJA Announcement

Today my heart is aflame with love. 

First, for my wife. There isn’t a sufficient word for what she has become to me. 

Her strength — how she she roared as she “thread the universe through the eye of a needle”. I couldn’t have admired you more, and yet I couldn’t have admired you enough without witnessing this birth.(Or the relentless feedings thereafter.)

You are not just Mom. You are some ascendant form of partner. Mother-of-my-child, sure. Co-pillar for new weight. Mom refers to her relationship to Naya, but not enough to your new relationship to me. It is both new and spectacular. 

Then, of course, for Naya, my girl. I have never been so exultant. This, even as your needs, for now, eclipse my own. There is no peace like you on my chest. No hope like what I dream for you. Most inspiring are those dreams you’ll dream I cannot now imagine. 

Above this, our new family has been so wholly enveloped by the love of our village. In these first days, we have gotten to peacefully inhabit our home, enjoying the delicacies of the world delivered by our generous families of origin and of choice. Thank you to all of you who are allowing us to cocoon into the most nourishing love bubble I have ever known. 

Welcome to the world, Naya Jean Abraham.
