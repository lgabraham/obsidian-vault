# On Money
Sometimes I get protective over preserving dollars for consumption. This seems short-sighted and selfish. 

But also, I intend to be, metaphorically, a man who comfortably stands in front of Wilson Hurley paintings. 

I think it is the attitude of lack that bothers me. I do not wish to self-abdicate, and I do wish to emphasize lifestyle. But I also understand I must be building for the future.
