# Yvonne Hawkins Review
Yvonne is resourceful, energetic, positive, and shrewd. She loves river-front property — her love was palpable every time she visited our place — and this love shows through in her work. She is deeply knowledgeable about the region, building an expertise in water-front sales. 

This latter part is important, because many other realtors  may dramatically undervalue property. Yvonne helped sell my property nearly 50% above what another realtor quoted me as “the upper limit” because she shared my vision for what our property could mean to the right buyer. 

Above all, Yvonne was a great partner through our sale process. She listened. We had to hustle to catch the tail end of the buying season, and she marshaled all of the resources to make this happen. Our sale was emotional, and she guided us with soft hands. We had a wide range of buyers — commercial, residential, local, out-of-state — and she could communicate with all of them. Our sale was complex, and she performed all the due diligence to ensure we could navigate this complexity. In the end, we set a “high water mark” for our price in our market.

I highly recommend Yvonne as your realtor.
