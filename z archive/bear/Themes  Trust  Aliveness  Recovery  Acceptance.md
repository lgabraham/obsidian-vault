# Themes:  Trust | Aliveness | Recovery | Acceptance

1. Acceptance - I am the art of letting go

“Letting go involves being aware of a feeling, letting it come up, staying with it, and letting it run its course without wanting to make it different or do anything about it.” - Hawkins

“Letting go is Hard.” - Lief

2. Trust - I trust completely 

Trust involves risking more with one another, and with oneself, and believing the relationship can handle the outcome. 

"The best way to find out if you can trust somebody is to trust them." - Hemingway 

3. Agency - I am the change I see

"High Agency is a sense that the story given to you by other people about what you can/cannot do is just that - a story. And that you have control over the story. High Agency person looks to bend reality to their will. They either find a way, or they make a way.” - Mack

“Life can be much broader once you discover one simple fact: Everything around you that you call life was made up by people that were no smarter than you and you can change it, you can influence it, you can build your own things that other people can use.” - Jobs
