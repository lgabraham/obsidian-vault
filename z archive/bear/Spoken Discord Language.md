# Spoken Discord Language

Spoken is a community dedicated to finding the best price on the web for a given item. 

We're happy you're here. Please engage in wanted behavior and accept our basic rules.

**Wanted Behavior**

1. Engage positively and proactively with other dupe hunters.
2. Share strategies, seek support, celebrate wins in your own dupe hunt.
3. Suggest improvements to the existing sites and processes.

**Unwanted Behavior**

1. **Zero tolerance** for hate speech, scamming, NSFW content, or toxic behavior.
2. Please do not send unsolicited messages to members 
3. Please refrain from spam and self-promotion

Discord Guidelines and ToS will be upheld in addition to our rules:  https://discord.com/guidelines

**Be scam-aware.**
Please protect yourself from spams and scamming: https://discord.com/safety/360044104071-Tips-against-spam-and-hacking

**Need help?**

If you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, let the staff know. Every Humanoid should feel welcome here!
