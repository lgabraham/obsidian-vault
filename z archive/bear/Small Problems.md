# Small Problems:

**How To Stay Calm (Without Any Hacks)**

- Overthinking → Write
- Uninspired → Read
- Scared → Take a risk
- Stuck → Walk
- Tired → Sleep
- Confused → Ask
- Frustrated → Move
- Burned out → Take a day off
- Impatient → Review progress
- Unmotivated → Remember your "why"
- Overwhelmed -> verbalize
