# SEO Goals
Emina on SEO P1s for Spoken

P1: Explore ways to preload anything on product pages without JS — at the very least, the H1 and first line of copy.
A reason we moved faster with the first site is that Google uses preloaded content. Google is only seeing gray squares today.

P1: Replace infinite scroll with pagination, or *compliment* infinite scroll with pagination to make it search-friendly. Add a “load more” button & <a> tag pagination.
Google doesn’t scroll. So adding a load more button w/ follow link will help.

There are more details on Google's Webmaster Guidelines for the pagination here (also linked in the spreadsheet) but basically Google can't/doesn't always scroll, and even if it does it likely won't infinitely scroll. Adding a load more button which has a followable link to a &?page=2 or whatever helps that cause.
https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2014/02/infinite-scroll-search-friendly
