# Zach

From the Iliad to the modern Olympic Games, the most epic contests of our history have involved lauded combatants crossing seas.  To witness such games is an honor. To participate, or so I imagine, a glory all its own. 

Please join me in honoring Zach’s journey across the Atlantic with a general’s welcome. And with much-anticipated, sublime and subversive battle in our first in-person Avalon game since before the pandemic. 

The venue is draw enough: Dhruv’s happy home and sky deck. The date is Tuesday January 25th. A school night, yes. But the memory will last your life time.
