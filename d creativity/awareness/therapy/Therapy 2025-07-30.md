# Needing vs. depending
It seems to be the needing that is difficult. Depending and needing are not the same. 

Depending brings up issues of trust for me. 

“Needing” cheapens dependency.  

I constrain my emotional aperture to try to front run missing. Taking something away before it is taken away from me.
