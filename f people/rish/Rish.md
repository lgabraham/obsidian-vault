Trying to formulate your superpower / blind spot from my perspective. Really is when I am most attracted to you versus when I am most averse. This is messy and over text. I trust. 

When you are in love (with the future, with an idea, with a friend, with a lover, with a moment), I find you absolutely magnetic. 

When you are in what I might affectionately call “mania”, I have an aversion to you. Mania ~~ audacious declarations without (to me) clear connection to reality. (Not that I am not also tickled by this, too.)

Love flows from self awareness, admiration, being in touch with yourself, the other, real details. I consider the compassionate challenge Andrew names and I would echo related to this. Also the warmth and authenticity.

“Mania” seems marked by seeming (to me) to be in a state of high emotion / activity, yet also out of touch with your own self or commitments. 

When you are in love, the power of your emotion illuminates the (truly incredible) machinery of your mind. When you are in “mania”, I find myself doubting what you say. 

It’s not always clear to me the difference. I also think “mania” can also be its own kind of differentiator and super power. You say something crazy and run to it. If I had to guess at the difference between these two “states”, it would be whether or not you are in  touch with your truth.

With love.
