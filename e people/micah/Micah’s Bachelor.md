Two thoughts came to mind for before dinner drinks. One is called Spygold, which is located inside and downstairs from a great restaurant near Times Square. The other is Raines Law Room at the William Hotel. Both might be able to offer something private or semi-private.

For after the show, definitely depends on the vibe, but some thoughts:

The Lobby Bar at the Chelsea Hotel - less raucous, more martinis 🍸

The Standard Biergarten - fun indoor/outdoor beer garden with ping pong tables at the Standard High Line hotel

Le Bain - famous club on the top floor of the Standard High Line Hotel with DJs

Bathtub Gin - cocktail bar with a DJ on Saturdays

Chez Zou - great cocktail bar on the top floor of the Pendry hotel
