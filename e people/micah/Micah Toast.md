To Micah and Tatiana.

1. On Micah

You spoke of being astounded by Tatiana’s qualities. I was astounded by yours. You have a poet’s heart, a philosopher’s mind, and a dark comic’s sense of humor.

4. On laughter 

We’d maniacally about something so twisted we couldn’t share it with anyone here. My wife has had to remind me many times when I float a joke with her “I’m not Micah”. You and I can laugh anywhere, our laughing until we cry is one of my favorite life activities, and one of my favorite memories of us is just the two of us howling at a nondescript strip mall pizza shop. 

I used to think I was really funny but then I realized that Micah is just really good at making me feel really funny. 

5. On friends

It turned out that you also would get along with all of my friends. You are so good natured that everyone falls in love with you. This is a great gift to me because you are so integrated into my life. I even invited you on our honey moon – to my wife’s surprise. 

6. On Naya

Even for as spectacular as our relationship is — the San Francisco to LA bike ride, the endless nights of games, Burning Man — watching you be so committed to bringing books into Naya’s life makes me realize there are yet more dimensions of our relationship to uncover. 

7. On being only children

So, Micah and I are only children. (I know, it’s obvious.) One of the features of only children is how well we share. So when I heard of Tatiana and noticed Micah starting to listen to Tatiana’s strong opinions rather than my own, I took it hard.

8. On meeting Tatiana

Tatiana, the first time I played Captain Sonar with you, I discovered first hand you can be intense. I wasn’t sure what to think. But I have had the privilege of forming my own relationship with you, which I truly treasure. Tanya, at every gathering you promote soulful and courageous conversation. And you’re just a ton of fun. And I witnessed how you loved my friend. And how he was better for this. 

9. On knowing Tatiana

And indeed your intensity is now my friend’s sword and shield. At Burning Man – which Tatania effortlessly fit into – when there was spectacular rain, Tatiana sprung into action digging a ditch around the tent to avoid water coming into it. Micah was “saving their spot” on the comfy couch in the common area.

10. On family
I was born with no siblings. Micah, you are a brother to me, and for life. While some might complain about not having siblings, how lucky were we to be able to choose one another? I plan to listen to you incessantly talk about watches deep into our old age. True siblings wouldn’t do that. 

11. On the future

For as long as I’ve known you, you have longed for familial love. Perhaps because you were so well loved by Daryl + Robert. 

Now look at all of this love you have made and are making. It is one of the great honores of my life to be apart of it. Here is to you. Mazel Tov.
