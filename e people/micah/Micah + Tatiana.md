# Micah + Tatiana

When I first heard of Tatiana, I felt protective of my friend. As an only child myself, I didn’t want anyone else playing with my friend. 

I’ve had the privilege to get to know Tatiana. And sometimes I even wish Micah wasn’t there. 

She takes care of him. X

Not unsurprisingly, being an only child can be a lonely road. It is one of the greatest gifts of my life that these only children found brotherhood in one another.
