## History & arc
The friendship is the bedrock — ~two decades deep. We built **Spoken** together (AI that cuts through hidden markup, "shopping in God-mode," starting with furniture), backed by Gradient (Google's AI fund) and YC.

**2024 was the crucible.** Heading into paternity leave, in an "existential window" for the company, I wrote Dane the hardest, clearest letters of our partnership: a boundary around being present for Mimosa, Naya, and Enso (fully offline July 10–26), and a **decision rule** — if we didn't close ~**$750k** of new capital (with him in for no more than a third), I couldn't continue. "This isn't an ultimatum — just clarity." And the deepest cut, the *hard truths*: "I'm not ready to follow the CEO you have been… I have learned not to hope that you change." We navigated it; **Spoken is still operating** (Spoken:Explore shipped in 2025).

Through all of it, I was also his friend in love and loss — I counseled him through whether to let **MK** go ("if the reason you can't say an enthusiastic yes is a belief you're not worthy of love, you're making a mistake"; Micah's "commit, and let your mind catch up"). They eventually parted; he's now with **Natasha**. And the friendship itself never broke — by **2026** we're still in near-daily contact, trading shorts and jokes and "Duuuude!!!", reading books together (Shackleton's *Endurance* with Micah), Warriors games at Chase Center.

## Candid reflections
This is the most complex relationship in here, and I want to hold it honestly. There's a **protective dynamic** I've questioned in myself: *"Did I spend a lot of time soothing him so that he would protect me? Then he did not protect me — he was not able to."* That ache is real and sits alongside enormous love.

Dane's **excellence** is its own thing — it "never feels like being amazing, just surprise that other people are bad at stuff," and he can be tickled by others' incompetence. I both admire his mind and feel the edge of that. The hardest truth I've named — that I couldn't follow *the CEO he'd been* — coexists with "building this with you has been one of the richest arenas of my life." Loving him has meant being able to hold a clear-eyed view *and* unconditional commitment at once: "whatever the case, I love you, my family loves you, and we are with you."
