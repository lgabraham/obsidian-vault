# Dane
Dane,

Before I head out on paternity leave, and after some reflection, I want to put into clear words where I am.

These uncertainty of the past months has been heavy (I know on us both). I’ve found myself losing hope that our business can support my family or justify the continued stress and cost. I also feel angry that we’re not further along before I leave — in part because it makes me feel like I have to choose between showing up for my family and showing up for you. I care deeply about both.

We’ve talked before about us both needing to set clear boundaries. I’m grateful you’ve invited this. I think my “line” is more around capitalization than revenue — that we have raised ~$750k and that you haven’t had to put in more than a 3rd of this. That’s what I need to continue — for myself and for my family.

Until I leave, I’m still in it with you. I’m willing to run at our goals and give it what I can before I go offline. But from July 10–26, I’ll be fully offline. We said we’d play and do the hard thing together — and for me, protecting this time with my family is part of that. It’s how I stay able to show up at all.

I know my presence motivates you, and that my absence might feel hard. I don’t take that lightly. I’m not leaving. I’m trying to stay in integrity with everything I care about. And if we reach a point where it’s time to let go, I trust we’ll face that with honesty and mutual respect.

Grateful for you, my friend.
Geoff
