**Waldenstrom macroglobulinemia (lymphoplasmacytic lymphoma)**

Lymphoplasmacytic lymphoma (LPL) is a rare type of cancer that accounts for just  [1 to 2 percent](https://www.cancer.org/cancer/non-hodgkin-lymphoma/about/types-of-non-hodgkin-lymphoma.html)  of all lymphomas. It mostly affects older adults. Waldenstrom macroglobulinemia is a subtype of LPL. It causes the abnormal production of antibodies.  [Many people with LPL have anemia; read more about other common symptoms and risk factors.](https://www.healthline.com/health/lymphoplasmacytic-lymphoma)
