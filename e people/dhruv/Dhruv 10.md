# Dhruv

Dhruv is the clearest thinker I know. Before I met him, I imagined that such raw intelligence would come at the cost of empathy or social confidence or moral virtue or humor. It does not. 

Our laughter is loud. Our conversations are rich. My admiration for his principles is unsurpassable. He truly has it all. And so it is always a gift to be in his presence in any capacity.
