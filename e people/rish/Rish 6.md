# Rish

One of the most surprising things about knowing you better is how others-centric you are. You are powerfully empathetic, and in tune with how others arrive, each in their own unique way, at psychological safety. I see it in your leadership style, and the art of your soft asks. Why surprising? Perhaps because you can also be so unfiltered and unabashedly yourself. I imagine that your empathy comes from a place of principle. This happens to be another thing that I love about you — how your principles govern you.

A word that comes to mind when I think of you is “restless”. On the one hand, it is related to another super power of yours — this abiding desire not to satisfied. I see it now as you mull over how to build conviction for big bets. It has its own kind of wonder, perhaps for another email. I do worry for you that you will not get the rest you need to stay young in body and mind. As do I worry for you that what ever animates the restlessness will prevent you from arriving at a grounding center with a committed partner. These matter to me because while I don’t want you to lose the “restlessness,” I do want to see you thrive, and know what it’s like to experience your restlessness at 75. 

I wonder if you could offer the same empathy you offer others to yourself. I trust you can do this without losing your drive.

Love, 
G
