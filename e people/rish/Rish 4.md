# Rish

My friend, some things I believe that may be “hard truths”, ie things that are difficult to find time and space to say:

Personal meaning is deeply related to constraint. 

Partnership requires sacrifice. The only variable is whether it is willing. 

Meaning in partnership, romantic or otherwise, compounds. 

Secure love enables our subconscious to thrive. (You may underestimate this.)

Fear of commitment is an invitation to better understand yourself. 

None of these things imply a decision one way or another. And I love and support you now matter your path. 


Boundless freedom risks becoming shallow.
